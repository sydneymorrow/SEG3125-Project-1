Meet the team!

Our taleneted team consits of four dentists, four hygienists and our administration and clean up team. Our team comes from all across Canada to deliver quality dental services. Our staff is dedicated to ensuring the saftey and sanity of our patients and facilities. Following Covid-19 regulations, our staff is required to wear PPE at all times in the office.

Dentists

Dr. Steve Stevens
Prosthodontist
University of Toronto, PhD, 1979

Dr. Stevens completed his PhD in dentistry 1979 and completed his prosthodontist residency program in 1981. Dr. Stevens specializes in treating and handling dental and facial problems. Dr. Stevens has been in our Big Tooth Team since 2016.

Dr. Rebecca McBecca
Periodontist
University of Toronto, PhD, 1979

Dr. McBecca studied Periodontics at the University of Toronto and earned her PhD in 2003. She specializes in preventing, diagnosing, and treating gum disease. She also performs dental implant treatments and tooth extractions. Dr. McBecca has been on The Big Tooth team since 2012.

Dr. Bob Humphry
Dentist
University of Alberta, DDS, 1998

Dr. Humphry studied dentistry at the University of Alberta and earned his DDS in 1998. Dr. Humphry performs complete dental examiniations and preventative dentistry. Dr. Humphry specializes in working with children. Dr. Humphry has been on our team since 2008.

Dr. John Micheals
Dentist
McGill, DDS, 1995

Dr. Micheals studied General Dentistry at McGill university and graduated in 1995. Dr. Micheals founded The Big Tooth along with his husband Bradley Micheals in 2005. Dr. Micheals performs routine dental procedures; crowns, dentures, fillings and bridges.
